Brandon Sanderson

Epic Fantasy Best Seller



Nowadays, Brandon Sanderson is one of the fantasy writers with most renown around the world, being a New York Times Best Seller many times as this is redacted.

  • The Cosmere is the fictional universe he created for many of his fantasy novels to take place within. Currently Elantris, Mistborn, Warbreaker, The Stormlight Archive, White Sand, and anything from Arcanum Unbounded are part of the Cosmere.
  • This idea came from Sanderson's desire to create an epic-length series without requiring readers to buy a ridiculous number of books. Because of that, he hides connections to his other works within each book, creating a "hidden epic". He has estimated that the Cosmere sequence could conclude with at least 40 books.
  • He created Sanderson's Laws of Magic and popularized the idea of "hard magic" and "soft magic" systems.
  • In 2008, Sanderson started a podcast with author Dan Wells and cartoonist Howard Tayler called Writing Excuses, involving topics about creating genre writing and webcomics.
